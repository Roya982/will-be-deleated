Sleep time lets us rest, restore, repair, and it may even keep our brains young and “plastic.” Understanding the importance of sleep is a key step in knowing how to sleep better.

Stages of Sleep

  1. Stage 1. Light sleep, easy to wake. 5–10 min.
  2. Stage 2. Light sleep, preparing for deep sleep. 10–15 min.
  3. Stage 3. Deep sleep. Hard to wake. Body repairs itself and boosts the immune system.
  4. REM sleep. Rapid eye movements. Your brain clears plaques in this stage. 10 minutes to 1 hour. REM cycles get longer as the night progresses.

Sleep Disorders

  • Insomnia.
  • Fragmented sleep.
  • Trouble falling asleep.
  • Restless leg syndrome.
  • Sleep apnea.
  • Sleepwalking.
  • Night terrors.
  • REM sleep behavior disorder.
  • Teeth grinding.
  • Narcolepsy.
  • Circadian rhythm problems.
  • Nighttime muscle cramps.

Why Don’t We Sleep?

  • Choice.
  • Daytime caffeine.
  • Shift work.
  • Being a parent.
  • Certain diseases.
  • Not enough daylight.
  • Diet.

10 Tips for Better Sleep

  • Keep Clocks Out of Your Bedroom
  • Follow a Sleep Schedule
  • Get More Daylight
  • Have a Coffee Cutoff Time
  • Try Audiobooks
  • Distraction Techniques
  • Meditation and Meditation Apps
  • Mantras, Prayers, Breathing, and Mandalas.
  • Eat Well.
  • Exercise.
